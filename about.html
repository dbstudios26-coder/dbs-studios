Our story

Born in Guwahati.
Built in 2026.

DB Studios did not start in a fancy office or a commercial print shop. It started in February 2026 with a single Epson L4360 printer, a home desk in Guwahati, and a founder who believed that premium print quality should be accessible to everyone.

Bhargav D B Gogoi built DB Studios from the ground up — handling everything personally, from the first test print to the first real customer. By March 2026, word had spread. Local businesses, students, and event organisers started reaching out for posters, labels, and photo prints that actually looked professional.

In April 2026, DB Studios went online — giving customers across India the ability to order, upload files, and get prints delivered directly to their door via Shiprocket. What started as one printer is now a growing print studio with big plans.
