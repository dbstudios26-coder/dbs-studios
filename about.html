Our story

Born in Guwahati.
Built for everyone.

DB Studios started in 2024 with a clear frustration — getting professional-quality prints in Guwahati meant either paying too much, waiting too long, or settling for output that didn't meet expectations.

Bhargav D B Gogoi set out to change that. Starting from a home-based setup with a focus on precision and care, DB Studios has grown into a trusted print partner for local businesses, event organisers, students, and individuals across Assam.

Every order — big or small — gets the same level of attention. From a single photo print to a batch of promotional posters, we treat every job as if it were our own.
